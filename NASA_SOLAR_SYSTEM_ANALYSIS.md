## 3. Key Findings

- **Habitability**: Earth is the only 100% habitable planet. Mars shows a 30% habitability potential, making it the primary candidate for exploration.
- **Toxicity**: Most planets (except Earth and partially Mars) are 100% toxic to human life.
- **Satellite Distribution**: Outer planets (Jupiter and Saturn) hold the vast majority of natural satellites, with Saturn leading at 146.
- **Temperature Gradient**: There is a clear correlation between distance and temperature, ranging from 737K (Venus, due to greenhouse effect) to 44K (Pluto).
